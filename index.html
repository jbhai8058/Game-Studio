ABOUT THE GAME

Shadow Strike is an upcoming action-adventure game set in a vast fantasy world. Developed by a team of industry veterans, the game combines intense melee combat with deep RPG elements and a compelling narrative.

Players take on the role of a warrior blessed (or cursed) with shadow magic, struggling to uncover the truth behind their mysterious powers while battling against a corrupt empire.
